About Feed The Hungry

Feed The Hungry is a not-for-profit social organization working to solve hunger, malnutrition and food wastage in the country. We channelize extra food from individuals, weddings, restaurants, corporate offices to the people who really need it and have no means or access to food. We also serve freshly cooked food or raw grains to our partnered shelter homes and beneficiaries.
